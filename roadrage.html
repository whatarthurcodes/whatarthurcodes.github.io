Road Rage Detector

Road Rage Detector is an accident prevention system. 80% of drivers experience some form of road rage which greatly increases the chances of car accidents. The system works in two parts: detection & mitigation. In the detection component, a bio-sensor is integrated in to a car steering wheel in order to read for symptoms of anger such as elevated heart rate or muscle tension. In the mitigation component, music is played to help aid drivers in realizing and dispelling their anger.

My Role

Throughout the course of the project, my main role was to act as a Project Manager. I kept team members on track in order to meet our deadlines. I lead the brain storming and ideation sessions in order to create different unique solutions.
