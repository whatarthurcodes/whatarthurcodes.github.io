UX Design

226-GAR-BAGE

Problem: Citizens of Stratford often forget to put out their waste. Furthermore they can't remember whether if its recycling, trash or organic waste.

Solution: 226-Gar-bage is a SMS based service that sends messages to remind users of when and what their garbage collection schedule is. Users simply sign up by sending in their adress, afterwards they will be reminded the night before of what type of waste (recycling, gargage, green waste) to put out. Furthermore, the service can provide information about trash that users may not know how to dispose of. Check ou the mock-up sample shown on an iphone.

Advantages: Solutions that others came to invovled building a mobile application. This is a fundamental problem as text messaging is a more widely used technology. It is not restricted to age group as senior citizens will still have the knowledge to use this technology. Lastly users without a smart phone are not excluded.

Arthur's Role: Brain storming idea to utilize text messaging over creating an mobile app. Creating reply text-messages to make service easy to use and intuitive.

Skills and Technologies used: User Centered Thinking, Adobe Illustrator, MS Powerpoint

uXperience Design Camp is a unique learning expereience hosted by the University of Waterloo to give students the opportunity to create a viable solution to a real problem utilzing methodologies based on UX.
